How do Robot Mowers make your yard healthier and more beautiful?

Robot mowers make your lawn healthier and more beautiful than conventional mowing by cutting more frequently and by cutting less off the grass blades at a time. This has been determined to cause the roots of the grass to go deeper into the soil so when you have a dry season, your grass stays greener than your neighbors. Only a small amount of the blade is remp4ed at a time, but more frequently. This results in less matting of large clippings and less shocking of the blade by cutting too much off at a time. By consistently mowing small amounts your yard stays healthier and greener.

Additionally, robot mowers can mow in neat rows that you can set the direction and pattern for in the smartphone app. This is really a no-brainer!
